About the team
The Technology Strategy and Transformation (TS&T) practice works closely with CxOs at the intersection of business and technology to articulate and solve key business problems, leveraging technology. The focus is to maximize business value from IT investments, drive organizational performance, and help our clients become more agile & productive.

Our team brings an independent and technology agnostic perspective across all aspects of the technology function and the business-technology lifecycle, ensuring alignment and readiness to serve the overall business vision. Our people include cross-industry professionals, ex-CIOs, and tech experts supported by powerful assets and robust frameworks.

Relevant skills:

1 to 3 years of experience in consulting, business analysis, or technology strategy, with a strong understanding of business and IT alignment.
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Business Technology Management, Information Systems, Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related field, with demonstrated ability to bridge business and technology domains.
Passionate about modern technology and their applications in the business world.
An analytical and problem-solving mindset, with the ability to break down complex problems, conduct root cause analysis, and develop actionable recommendations.
Comfortable working in ambiguous environments, with the ability to interpret business needs and translate them into clear requirements and solutions.
Proven ability to manage multiple priorities, work collaboratively in team settings, and deliver high-quality outputs under tight timelines.
Effective presentation and storytelling skills, with the ability to synthesize data and insights into compelling narratives for business audiences.
Proficiency in PowerPoint and Excel, including experience building dashboards, business cases, and executive-level reports.
Proficiency in utilizing AI tools (e.g., generative AI, automation platforms, data analysis tools) to enhance productivity and insights development is considered a strong asset.
Continuous learner mindset, with a passion for upskilling in digital tools, methodologies, and industry trends.
Ability to elicit, document, and validate business and functional requirements, and collaborate with technical teams to ensure alignment with business goals.
Familiarity with IT service domains, such as cloud computing, infrastructure, enterprise applications, and digital workplace technologies.
Knowledge of how a Technology (or IT) function operates, engages with the business, IT portfolio management lifecycle (e.g., SDLC), latest operating model trends (i.e., product and platform models, scaled agile)
